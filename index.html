Book Your Adventure Now!

Read the terms below and click book now when ready:

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all our hikers, please review our detailed booking terms. Reservations must be made at least 48 hours in advance to secure your spot and are subject to availability. Upon booking, you will receive a confirmation email with all necessary details regarding your hike. Cancellations made more than 24 hours prior to the scheduled hike will receive a full refund, ensuring flexibility for your plans. Unfortunately, cancellations made within 24 hours of the hike are non-refundable, due to the preparation and logistics involved. In the event of inclement weather or unforeseen circumstances, we reserve the right to reschedule or cancel hikes; in such cases, a full refund or alternative date will be offered. Participants must arrive at the meeting point at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time to allow for a briefing and equipment check. All hikers are required to adhere to our safety guidelines and must sign a liability waiver before embarking on any hike. This ensures that everyone is aware of the necessary precautions and can enjoy the adventure safely.
